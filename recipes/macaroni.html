Macaroni Recipe

Description

Macaroni and cheese also called mac and cheese in Canada and the United States and macaroni cheese in the is a dish of cooked macaroni pasta and a cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 8 ounce of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pot of lightly salted water. Cook Macaroni in water.
  2. At the same time, melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 min.
  4. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring. Continue to cook until mix is smooth.
  5. Add cheddar cheese until melted.
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
