Chicken Marsala Recipe

Description

Chicken Marsal is an Italian-American dish of golden pan-fried chicken cutlets and mushrooms in a rich Marsala wine sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 and half pound of boneless chickn breast
  • 3 tablesppons all-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 3 saltec unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 (8-oz) package pre-slice bella or button mushrooms
  • 3 tablesppons finely chopped shallots, from 1 meduim shallot
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 2/3 cup chicken broth
  • 2/3 dry Marsala wine
  • cup heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons chopped frersh thyme
  • 2 taplespoons chopped fresh Italian parsely, for serving

Instructions

  1. Place the flour, 3/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in ziplock bag.
  2. Add the chicken to the bag; seal bag tightly and shake to coat chicken evenly.Set aside.
  3. Heat the oil and add 2 tablespoons of the butter in large skillt over medium-high heat.
  4. Place the flour-dusted chicken in the pan, shaking off any excess first, and cook,turning once, until the chicken is golden.5-6 min total.
  5. Transfer the chicken to a plate and set aside.
  6. Melt the remaining tablespoon of butter in the pan.
  7. Add the mushroom and cook,stirring frequently, until the mushrooms begin to brown, 3 to 4 minutes.
  8. Add the shallots, garlic, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt;cook for 1 to 2 minutes more.
  9. Add the broth, Marsala,heavy cream, thyme,1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon of pepper;use a wooden spoon to scrape any brown bits from pan to liquid
  10. Bring liquid to boil, then reduce the heat to medium and gently boil, until sauce is reduced by half,thickened, and darkned in color, 10 to 15 min
  11. Add the chicken back to pan, reduced the heat to low and simmer until chicken is warmed. 2 to 3 min. Sprinkle with parsely, and serve.
